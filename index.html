Puntawat Ponglertnapakorn

I am a Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Prof. Supasorn Suwajanakorn in the Vision and Learning Lab (VLL) at VISTEC, Thailand. My research focuses on applying deep learning and generative models to face relighting, human motion generation for football players, and computer vision for sports virtual replay and reconstruction. I’m passionate about exploring how these approaches can be applied to the football domain, including modeling player movements and recreating virtual replay experiences.
